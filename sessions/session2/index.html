Session 2: The Road Ahead

April 8, 2026 · 3 min read

Players: Merla, Cordelia

Session Recap​

Left in the cold dank warehouse deep in Ruarden Hold, Merla confronted with Duggle heard from him a cryptic rhyme - some kind of warning. Moments later, two dwarves entered the warehouse, forcing Merla and Cordelia into hiding.

As they moved through the halls, they overheard Cecilia ordering a full search of the hold after she discovered what had happened to Trudie, her daughter, and discovering that their rooms were empty. Managing to avoid detection, the players made it to the top of the hold, where Cordelia tripped, alerting a dwarf in the process. Duggle appeared again, this time distracting dwarves, allowing the party to narrowly escape.

Beyond the hold, the Balguard mountains, now plump with fresh snow from the previous night's blizzard, proved treacherous. The steep terrain paired with the slick ground made the descent dangerous... Cordelia slipped down the mountain side and injured herself, while Merla who was subtly warned by Duggle, managed to navigate the mountain-face with greater ease.

Eventually the party made it from the mountains into a dense woodland, and eventually to the lakeside village of Cloverstead. There they learned of disappearances in a town far away called "Ravenwater," and that Henry Greenwich's body had been recovered from there - with the person who found him due to appear before the high court of Venmeer.

That night brought little rest. Duggle appeared again to Merla as she settled into bed, he hinted at lingering magical scars - something haunting her, and offered to listen to whatever burden she carried. In another room, Cordelia experienced a vivid and deeply personal vision in a sleep, Theo appearing to her. Upon waking, she realised another memory had slipped from her grasp, the name of her lover.

Both awoke changed, with a stronger resolve than ever before. Making it to level 2.

On the outskirts of Cloverstead, Merla and Cordelia came across a run down house marked with the same insignia found in the basement of Ruarden Hold. Upon their approach they saw wagons outside and the building was heavily guarded by dwarven guards.

Continuing along the road, the party came upon a grisly scene. A man laid torn apart near a damaged wagon, and a monstrous, decaying creature feasted upon a horse further down the road. After a dangerous confrontation, the beast fled.

Hidden within the wagon, they discovered a terrified mother and her young son. The pair had been travelling to Venmeer in search of a new life when they stopped to help what they thought was a stranger in trouble, only to be attacked.

With only one horse capable of pulling the wagon, Merla and Cordelia devised the idea to take the mother and son to Venmeer, having to leave half of their possessions behind while Cordelia took the reigns and Merla walked alongside the wagon.

Further along the road, the party encountered a travelling bard who offered songs for a coin or two. Choosing not to engage, the players ignored the bard incident.

What felt like many days later, the players had finally reached Venmeer, a sprawling port city and a hub for trade. Towering above the rooflines, the Arlington School of magic loomed, it's presence felt across the metropolis.

The mother and child parted ways with the party upon arrival, offering what little they could in thanks.

Though the journey had been long, Venmeer had little to offer in the way of respite.
