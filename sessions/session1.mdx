**Players**: Merla, Cordelia
## Session Recap
Following separate leads tied to forbidden magic, Merla and Cordelia both travelled into the icy Balguard mountains and sought shelter from an encroaching blizzard at **Ruarden Hold**, after being met with at the gate of the hold by a small dwarven child called **"Trudie."**

{/* truncate */}

Inside the mess hall, the dwarves grew openly hostile at any mention Umeå, also known as Stubsy. After being offered rooms for the night by Trudie, an encounter with her turned tense after the players attempted to intimidate her, drawing the attention of the dwarven soldiers. They successfully defused the situation, however, before settling in for the night. Later, the players awoke to the sound of a scream and began exploring the stronghold. They uncovered letters that revealed the internal collapse of **The Stonebloods**. Their previous leady, **Henry Greenwich** had been ousted with **Cecilia** taking control, and **Umeå** had become a liability due to his 'carelessness.' 

In the lower cells of the hold, the players found Umeå who was beaten, starving and barely clinging to life. Bitter and resigned, he directed the pair towards a hidden warehouse beyond a loose stone marked with a distinctive insignia. 

Inside the warehouse, a minor phantom attacked. After defeating it, Merla attempted to free a caged dragonborn, triggering a magical explosion that knocked her unconscious. When she awoke instead of who she thought she freed, Merla instead found a gnome called Duggle Grimsby. Enraged, she tried to attack the gnome that promised to stay with her, insisting that he saw her as a hero. Meanwhile, Cordelia opened a jewellery box containing a ring that spoke to her in her lover's voice. Upon wearing it, the ring fused to her finger, stealing the memory of her falling in love with her ex-lover, Theo, and leaving her with the eery feeling she may lose more. A note inside the ring's box read: "Bento's Bazaar, Venmeer" - a lead.
